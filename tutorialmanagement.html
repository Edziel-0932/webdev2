What to do with electronics that contain
brass?

Brass often found in connectors, plugs, and circuit boards.

Safety tips
Wear gloves and safety glasses when dismantling.
Don’t burn or melt brass at home leave processing to recyclers.

What to do with electronics that contain
copper?

Copper often found in  Wires and cables. And circuit boards Transformers, motors, and coils. Connectors and some plugs

Safety tips
Use proper tools (wire strippers, pliers) when recovering copper.
Don’t burn wires to remove insulation (it’s toxic).
Wear gloves to avoid cuts from sharp wires or boards.
